Frigorifico Guarani of interesting news.

Frigorífico Guaraní launches its new sausage grill XXL, which will be 210 grams and slightly spicy special to share also relaunched its famous Morcillas, with its characteristic flavor. Glenda Munoz, Frigorifico Guarani Brand Manager, said the company aims at the constant innovation of its products to be always present in the grills of Paraguayan families, based on consumer insight, true to its policy of being the best choice in Sausages with certified quality and appropriate price.

These announcements took place at the launch of the new "Proba", with which Frigorifico Guarani, invites you to discover the variety of products available to consumers.

The event was held on November 18 at the local Punto Carne, Where guests and friends of the press had the opportunity to taste appetizers prepared with different sausages.

Frigorifico Guarani proudly Paraguayan is a company with more than 30 years, innovation and quality leader in national and international market being the first company to make export of boneless meat products. In the exclusive sausage plant it has a installed production capacity of 600 tons per month and it provides more than 900 sources of work interacting with the community socially responsible manner.
